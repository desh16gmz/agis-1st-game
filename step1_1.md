# Game Concept: **ShiftBlocks**

## Genre
2D Puzzle‑Platformer blending falling‑block mechanics (à la Tetris) with character‑based platform navigation.

## Core Gameplay Loop
1. **Spawn Phase** – Randomly generated blocks fall from the top of the screen in a grid‑aligned column.
2. **Manipulation Phase** – The player can:
   - Move left/right, jump, and climb onto falling blocks.
   - Rotate or shift blocks horizontally while airborne or standing on them.
3. **Construction Phase** – As blocks settle, they become solid platforms. The player uses them to:
   - Reach higher platforms.
   - Solve spatial puzzles (activate switches, create paths).
4. **Clear Phase** – Completing a row of blocks (full horizontal line) clears that row, rewarding the player with:
   - Score boost.
   - Temporary abilities (e.g., double jump, slow fall).
5. **Repeat** – New blocks continue to fall, increasing speed/difficulty over time.

## Unique Twists
- **Dual‑Control Mechanic**: Player controls both a **character** and a **cursor**. The cursor can “grab” and reposition any settled block (once per level) to reshape the environment.
- **Dynamic Gravity Zones**: Certain zones invert gravity, causing blocks and the player to fall upward, creating multi‑directional platforming.
- **Morphing Blocks**: Some blocks change type after a set time (e.g., solid → bouncy → disappearing), forcing the player to adapt routes on the fly.
- **Narrative Integration**: Each cleared line unlocks a fragment of a story displayed in a stylized comic panel, encouraging progression beyond pure score chasing.

## Player Goals
- Reach the exit door at the top of each stage.
- Achieve high scores by clearing rows efficiently.
- Collect story fragments to unlock optional challenges and alternate endings.

## Progression & Difficulty
- **Level‑Based**: Each level introduces new block types, gravity zones, or environmental hazards.
- **Speed Ramp**: Fall speed increments every few cleared rows.
- **Puzzle Complexity**: Later stages require precise timing of block manipulation and dual‑control usage.

## Summary
*ShiftBlocks* merges classic falling‑block strategy with active platforming, giving players the agency to build, modify, and traverse a constantly evolving world. The combination of character movement, block manipulation, and environment‑changing mechanics offers a fresh take on the puzzle‑platform genre.
